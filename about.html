About Me

My journey in photography has been going on for over 5 years. What began as a simple fascination with the aesthetics of a moment has, over time, become my main hobby and a way to explore the world around me more deeply. Throughout these years, I have evolved from taking casual amateur shots to working mindfully with light and composition, dedicating my free time to studying various genres and techniques.

This passion has taught me the most important lesson: technology is merely a tool, and the true value of a frame lies in its ability to convey a sincere emotion or a special atmosphere of a moment. For me, every photo shoot is not a job, but a creative process where I can apply the knowledge I've gathered over the past 5 years to capture a story exactly as I see it.
